# NeoEmployee Strategy

## Current Phase: Bootstrap

We are a bootstrapped startup. No bank credits, no investors — full stop. This is a deliberate choice that shapes everything we do.

Our runway depends entirely on the work we generate. As a result, **anything that brings in money and aligns with our vision, we do.** Keeping the pipeline full is not just a sales goal, it is a survival constraint.

## Business Model Today: Time & Material

We operate as a custom software consultancy, building AI agents on a time-and-material basis. Every engagement is scoped and billed individually. This model generates the cash flow that keeps us running and funds our path toward products.

## The Dream: Products

Like any startup, our long-term goal is to build products — AI agents that are not custom-built for a single client but can be deployed across many different companies. We do not know if or when we get there, but it is the direction we are heading.

## The Bridge: Pattern Recognition

We currently have **2 of our 14 people** dedicated in the background to finding common ground across our customer base. Their job is to identify recurring patterns — skills, workflows, and agent types that appear across multiple clients in different industries. These patterns are the seeds of future products: non-custom, reusable agents built on real-world demand rather than speculation.

## Constraints That Shape Our Decisions

- **No external funding** — no bank credits, no investor rounds, no dilution.
- **Revenue is runway** — every deal we close extends how long we can operate and invest in the product path.
- **Vision filter** — we pursue any revenue-generating opportunity that fits within our vision of building AI agents that replace employee skills. We do not chase work outside that frame.
