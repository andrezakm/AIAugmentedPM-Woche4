# NeoEmployee

## What We Do

NeoEmployee builds custom AI agents that replace employees or employee skills within organizations.

## How We Work

We engage with companies as consultants. Our process:

1. **Identify bottlenecks** — We go into companies and analyze their operations to find the worst bottlenecks.
2. **Identify skill gaps** — We determine which skills are missing or insufficiently covered.
3. **Build the agents** — We develop custom AI agents tailored to address those specific gaps.

## Business Model

- **Time & material** — We bill on a time-and-material basis.
- **Fully custom** — Every agent is built custom for the client; we do not sell AI agents as off-the-shelf products.
- **No products (yet)** — We are not currently building AI agents as products, only as custom software solutions.
